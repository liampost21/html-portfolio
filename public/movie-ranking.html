The Best Movies According to Liam

My top 5 movies of all-time

Last Night in Soho

Good soundtrack and 60s vibe. Great acting too from the two female leads.

Kill Bill Vol 1 & 2

Great violent action scenes and a great revenge story.

The Dark Knight

I love superhero films and this is the best Joker performance (or maybe any performance) that you will ever see.

Pearl

Amazing performance from female lead, so many quotes in the film too. Great character in Pearl. I also really like the colours in this film.

Avengers Endgame

Classic Marvel action and has the best superhero war scene ever.
